Welcome! I am Chujie Zheng ("Chujie" pronounced similarly to "Cho-Jay"), a fourth-year Ph.D student in [CoAI Group](http://coai.cs.tsinghua.edu.cn/) at Tsinghua University, fortunately advised by [Prof. Minlie Huang](http://coai.cs.tsinghua.edu.cn/hml/). I am also currently a visiting scholar in [PlusLab](https://vnpeng.net/) at UCLA, fortunately hosted by [Prof. Nanyun (Violet) Peng](https://vnpeng.net/). I received my B.Sc degree from Dept. of Physics, Tsinghua University. You can find my [**CV here** (English)](./cv_chujie_en.pdf).

I have a broad research interest in **building trustworthy NLP systems**, with the current focus on **<font color=red>LLM alignment and safety</font>**. I am working on understanding the intrinsic working mechanisms and limitations of modern LLMs and improving them to align with human expectations and social norms. My ultimate goal is to advance and oversee AI systems in a scalable way and ensure they operate transparently and responsibly.

Previously, I conducted research on **LLMs for social good**, with a main focus on building LLMs for emotional support. I have also built a series of **popular NLP datasets**, including [**ChID**](https://github.com/chujiezheng/ChID-Dataset), [**KDConv**](https://github.com/thu-coai/KdConv), [**ESConv**](https://github.com/thu-coai/Emotional-Support-Conversation), and [**CDConv**](https://github.com/thu-coai/CDConv), and am proud of them stimulating follow-up work!
